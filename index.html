Week 01: Speed & Readability

  • 'Users Love Fast Websites: Speed is the heart of UX; slow loads lose users.'
  • 'Fonts Affect Readability: Typography determines how fast information is absorbed.'
  • 'Mobile Design is Essential: Interfaces must be optimized for smaller screens.'
★

Week 02: Choice & Trust

  • 'Too Many Choices Confuse: Simplifying options improves overall usability.'
  • 'Consistency Builds Trust: Repeated patterns reduce confusion.'
  • 'Good Navigation Feels Invisible: Logical structure helps users find info naturally.'
★

Week 03: Action & Feedback

  • 'Button Placement Matters: Call-to-actions must be where users naturally expect them.'
  • 'Feedback Improves Confidence: Animations reassure users that their action worked.'
  • 'Visual Communication: Images and icons communicate faster than long text.'
